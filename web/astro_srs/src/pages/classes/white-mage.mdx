# White Mage

The White Mage is a devoted healer and protector, specializing in the art of white magic. They possess unparalleled abilities to restore vitality, cure ailments, and shield allies from harm. With their compassionate hearts and deep connection to the healing arts, White Mages are invaluable support pillars in any group, ensuring the well-being and survival of their comrades through the most dire of circumstances.
