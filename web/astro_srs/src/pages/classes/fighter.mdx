# Fighter

The Fighter is a stalwart warrior, honed in the art of armed combat. Armed with an array of weapons, from mighty swords to versatile axes, they are skilled in delivering powerful strikes and holding the line against any threat. Fighters excel in close-quarters combat, exhibiting exceptional strength, resilience, and tactical expertise. Their unwavering resolve and mastery of various fighting techniques make them the epitome of martial prowess.
