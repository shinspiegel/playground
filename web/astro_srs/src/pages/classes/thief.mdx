# Thief

The Thief is a nimble and elusive rogue, relying on stealth, agility, and cunning to overcome their adversaries. With unmatched dexterity and a keen eye for opportunities, they excel at ambushing foes, disarming traps, and pilfering valuable treasures. Thieves are proficient in close-quarters combat, using their quick reflexes and precision strikes to exploit their enemies' weaknesses, making them a formidable force both in and out of combat.
