# Monk

The Monk is a disciplined warrior who eschews weapons in favor of mastering the art of unarmed combat. Through rigorous training and spiritual enlightenment, they harness their inner energy, known as ki, to unleash lightning-fast strikes, devastating kicks, and awe-inspiring acrobatics. Monks embody agility, speed, and precision, effortlessly weaving through battles with their unmatched martial prowess.
