# Spellcasting

- No cantrip/zero level spell
- Spell needs to be more "magic"

## Slot magic

- Slot per level, need to decide the spell when prepare it
- Needs to prepare amounts of spell per day

## Mana magic

- Free caster
- Can cast any spell, just pay the spell cost
