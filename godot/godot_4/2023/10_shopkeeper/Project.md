# Game Flow

1. Crafting items
   - Potion (Heal)
   - Bombs (Damage)
   - Decoy (Distract?)
   - Tools (Collect better item)
1. Sell Items
   - Sell items in the shop display (needs to add the item and price)
1. Go to the Dungeon
   - Collect ingredients from ground
   - Use tools to collect better quality item
   - Kill enemies (and kill item)
